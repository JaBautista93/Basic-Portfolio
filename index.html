About Me

John-Anthony has dedicated his life to being a public servant. Since the age of 17, John-Anthony served in the United States Marines with 5 years of Active Duty and 1 year in the Reserves. John-Anthony currently works for the Federal Aviation Administration, responsible for Quality Management Program for the Compliance and Airworthiness Division. John-Anthony holds a Green Belt in Lean Six Sigma, is a Certified Change Management Practitioner, and has a background in Aerospace Engineering and Manufacturing. Outside work, John-Anthony has created a life of eating healthy and has given up sugars and maintains a low carb diet. He likes to think of himself as amateur chef and his inspiration to create healthy food comes from his Father and Cousin who are a successful chefs. John-Anthony is passionate about his communities and loves to coach and mentor, He couldn’t be this fortunate in life if he didn’t have his Mother and Girlfriend who is to have his biggest cheerleaders in life.
