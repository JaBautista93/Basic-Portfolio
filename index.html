About Me

John-Anthony has dedicated his life to being a public servant. Since the age of 17, John-Anthony served in the United States Marines with 5 years of Active Duty and 1 year in the Reserves. John-Anthony was an Quality Assurance Inspector and Avionics Maintainer for the Marines who had the opportunity to work on KC-130J Hercules aircraft and fly around the world. John-Antony also spent a deployment overseas managing the day shift for Avionics shop. They were the first team to work on open fuel cells in Iraq. Today John-Anthony works for the Federal Aviation Administration, responsible for the Quality Management Program for the Compliance and Airworthiness Division. John-Anthony holds a Green Belt in Lean Six Sigma, is a Certified Change Management Practitioner. John-Anthony is responsible to 15 offices across 4 time zones to ensure that the FAA maintains compliance to their processes and procedures. John-Anthony also has a background in operations for Aerospace Engineering and Manufacturing companies.

Outside work, John-Anthony has created a life of eating healthy and has given up sugars and maintains a low carb diet. He likes to think of himself as amateur chef and his inspiration to create healthy food comes from his Father and Cousin who are a successful chefs. When John-Anthony isn't cooking, he loves to read and visit historic sites, go ghost hunting, and can be caught watching some old school Saturday Cartoons.

John-Anthony is passionate about his communities and loves to coach and mentor, He couldn’t be this fortunate in life if he didn’t have his Mother and Girlfriend who is to have his biggest cheerleaders in life. John-Anthony credits all his success to his family and die-hard frends who have supported him throughout all of his adventures in life. He is greatful and humbled by the people around him. John-Anthony would love to teach and travel after his retirement in public service.
